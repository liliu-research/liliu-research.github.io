# Li Liu

**Marie Skłodowska-Curie Actions Postdoctoral Fellow**  
Electronic Components, Technology and Materials Group (ECTM) 
Delft University of Technology

## About me

I am a Marie Skłodowska-Curie Actions Postdoctoral Fellow in the Electronic Components, Technology and Materials group at Delft University of Technology.

My research focuses on advanced electronic packaging, with particular interests in die-attachment materials and technologies, interfacial reliability, multi-physics reliability modelling, and advanced materials characterisation.

My current MSCA project, **OrganVOID**, aims to develop antioxidant Cu sintering nanopastes for low-temperature, pressureless, and air-compatible die attachment in next-generation power electronic modules.

## Research interests

- Advanced electronic packaging
- Die-attachment materials and technologies
- Cu/Ag sintering
- Interfacial reliability
- Multi-physics reliability modelling
- X-ray CT/DVC-based characterisation
- Advanced packaging and heterogeneous integration
